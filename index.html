Captain's Rules

OBJECTIVE

Be the first to reach the Final Square.

TURN SEQUENCE

  1. Roll the D6.
  2. Move forward.
  3. Apply the square's effect.

SQUARE TYPES

  • EMPTY: No effect.
  • QUIZ: Answer a question (Marketing, Digital Creation, Web Dev).
    • Correct: Draw 1 Bonus Card.
    • Wrong: Move back 1 square.

BONUS CARDS

  • BOOST: Move forward, roll again, etc.
  • PROTECTION: Cancel penalties.
  • ASSET: Strategic advantages.
  • PENALTY: Attack opponents.
  • DUEL: Defend against penalties.
