## OBJECTIVE

Be the first to reach the **Final Square** on the board.

## GAME CONTENTS

- 1 Board (20 to 24 squares: Empty + Quiz)
- 1 8-sided Die (D8)
- Quiz Cards Deck (Marketing / Digital Creation / Web Development)
- Bonus Cards Deck
- 4 Pawns (for 4 players)

## SETUP

1.  Each player chooses a pawn and places it on the **Start** square.
2.  Shuffle the **Quiz Cards** and **Bonus Cards** separately.
3.  The youngest player starts, then play proceeds clockwise.

## TURN SEQUENCE

1.  Roll the **D8**.
2.  Move forward by the number indicated.
3.  Apply the effect of the square you land on.

## SQUARE TYPES

### 1. EMPTY Square

No effect. Wait for the next turn.

## QUIZ CATEGORIES

- **Marketing:** Key concepts, social media, branding, strategies, B2B/B2C communication, etc.
- **Digital Creation:** DTP, typography, colors, layout, Adobe software, formats, composition.
- **Web Development:** HTML, CSS, JS, front/back, hosting, code basics, APIs.

## BONUS CARDS

When a player answers a Quiz correctly, they draw one card from these 5 types:

### 1. BOOST Cards (Immediate positive effects)

- Move forward +2 squares.
- Roll again immediately.
- Advance to the next Quiz square.

### 2. PROTECTION Cards (Defensive effects)

- Cancel the next penalty received.
- Immunity for 1 turn.
- Prevent a player from making you move back.

### 3. ASSET Cards (Strategic advantages)

- Peek at the next question before answering.
- Choose the category of the next Quiz.
- Swap positions with a player behind you.

### 4. PENALTY Cards (Attack opponents)

- Force another player back 1 square.
- Make a player lose their next Bonus card.
- Send a player back to the last Empty square they passed.

### 5. DUEL Cards (Defense only)

- Used when another player gives you a penalty.
- Triggers a quick "Duel" (mini-quiz) between you two.
- If you win, the penalty is cancelled.

## FINAL SQUARE

When you reach or pass the **Final Square**:

1.  You must answer a **Final Quiz**.
2.  **Correct Answer:** You **WIN** the game!
3.  **Wrong Answer:** Move back **1 square**.

## DURATION

With 4 players and a D8: **20 to 30 minutes**.

## END OF GAME

The first player to succeed in their Final Quiz on the Final Square wins the game.
